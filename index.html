Doctor Recruitment

    Our criteria:

    In the UK it is a requirement to be registered with the General Medical Council (GMC) before being allowed to practice medicine.
    All overseas doctors must possess an acceptable primary medical qualification (PMQ) in order to apply for registration. 
    In most cases, it is mandatory for overseas doctors to undertake the Professional and Linguistic Assessment Board (PLAB) before commencing any NHS jobs or practising as a doctor in the UK.
    Registration with the GMC is usually obtained through:
    a) sitting the PLAB exam
    b) through having a recognised higher qualification – a list can be found here.

      You must also meet English language requirements by passing one of the following English language exams:

    • IETLS test (Doctors need to achieve an IELTS score of 7.5 overall with a minimum score of 7.0 in each testing area of speaking,
      listening, reading and writing)
    • OET test  (Doctors need to take the medicine version of the test and get a grade B in each testing area of speaking, listening,
      reading and writing)
    If you are interested, please click the Apply Now button and submit your details. We will review your information and advise of next steps

Other Healthcare Staff Recruitment
